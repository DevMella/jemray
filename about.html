About

Jemray Solar

Introduction

Jemray Solar is the renewable energy division of Jemray Nigeria Limited, dedicated to delivering clean, affordable, and dependable solar power solutions across Nigeria and West Africa and globally. With a strong focus on both off-grid and on-grid energy systems, we empower homes, businesses, and institutions through innovative technologies, including flexible Pay-As-You-Go (PAYGo) offerings and Productive Use of Renewable Energy (PURE) appliances

Our Mission

To provide affordable, accessible, and sustainable solar energy solutions to underserved communities, improving quality of life while supporting environmental sustainability and energy inclusion

Our Vision

To become an industry leader in renewable energy by making clean power solutions available to everyone — from off-grid communities to urban centers — through scalable technology, strategic partnerships, and customer-focused service.

Our Approach

We combine deep local knowledge with proven technical expertise to design solutions that are:

  • Flexible in financing
  • Durable in performance
  • Inclusive in impact
  • Scalable for long-term energy growth

Whether partnering with households or institutions, Jemray Solar delivers energy that transforms lives and enables sustainable development.
