Solarun CEO Visits Jemray Solar, Strengthening Strategic Ties for Off-Grid Expansion

Jemray Solar was honored to welcome Susan Lee, CEO of Solarun, to its Lagos office as part of ongoing efforts to deepen cooperation in delivering reliable solar energy solutions to underserved communities across West Africa. Read more

The strategic engagement featured in-depth discussions on improving product quality, after-sales support, and last-mile distribution. Both teams explored ways to better serve rural and peri-urban communities with solar home systems, including solar lanterns, fans, and cold storage units. Speaking during the visit, Engr.
