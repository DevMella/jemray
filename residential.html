Residential

Solar Solutions

Your Home, Comfortably Powered with Reliable Solar Energy

Jemray Solar delivers expertly designed residential solar systems to keep your home powered efficiently, day and night. Whether you live in urban centers or remote locations, our customizable solutions — typically ranging from 1kW to 20kW — ensure uninterrupted electricity, lower energy costs, and a sustainable lifestyle.

01

Tailored Residential Solar Systems

We match every system to your unique household energy needs, supporting everything from essential lighting and electronics to large appliances, pumps, and air conditioning.

02

Stylish Solar Carports

Maximize your compound’s space with solar carports that elegantly integrate solar panels, boosting power generation while protecting your vehicles

03

Backup & Hybrid Systems

Stay powered during grid outages with advanced hybrid systems combining solar, batteries, and smart inverters — delivering peace of mind and energy security.

04

Energy Storage Solutions

 Store excess energy with high-capacity battery systems scaled to your needs, ensuring 24/7 reliability even during extended cloudy or rainy days.

05

 Smart Monitoring & Controls

Track your solar production and household energy consumption in real-time through intuitive apps and dashboards, giving you full control over your system.

06

Reliable Customer Service

Our team provides professional installation, ongoing maintenance, technical support, and system upgrades — ensuring your solar investment performs optimally for years to come

Typical Technical Specs at a Glance:

  • System Size: 1kW – 20kW (customized to your home’s load requirements)
  • Battery Options: 2kWh – 40kWh (LFP or GEL batteries)
  • Inverter Types: Hybrid inverters up to 20kVA
  • Mounting Options: Rooftop, ground mount, or integrated solar carports
  • onitoring: Wi-Fi/4G-enabled smart monitoring
