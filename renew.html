Guiding Your Solar Projects with Expertise You Can Trust

At Jemray Solar, we don’t just install systems — we bring industry-leading expertise to help you plan, execute, and manage your renewable energy projects from start to finish. Whether you’re a homeowner seeking professional advice, a business aiming to transition to solar, or a government agency developing a large-scale energy program, Jemray Solar has the capacity to guide you every step of the way

01

Feasibility Studies & Site Assessments

Comprehensive evaluations of your site’s solar potential, expected yields, and technical requirements to ensure your project is viable and optimized for maximum performance.

02

System Design & Engineering Support

Detailed technical designs tailored to your energy goals, regulatory needs, and budget, including load analysis, component sizing, and optimal layout planning.

03

Project Management & Implementation Oversight

End-to-end coordination of your solar project’s lifecycle — from procurement to installation — ensuring timely delivery, adherence to standards, and cost-effectiveness.

04

Energy Audits & Efficiency Recommendations

Thorough assessments of current energy usage, with actionable strategies to reduce consumption and enhance your system’s return on investment.

05

Policy & Regulatory Advisory

Guidance on meeting local, national, and international renewable energy standards, incentives, and permitting processes.

06

Capacity Building & Training

Tailored workshops, training sessions, and educational materials for your team or community to maximize the benefits of your solar investment.
